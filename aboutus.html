Finding deals for you

DealHound simplifies the grocery shopping experience by collecting and curating deals from multiple retailers in one place. Add your grocery list and preferences, and our app will ensure that you have the best deals conveniently in hand. Competitors either act as a glorified flyer service or require you to hand over your entire shopping process for them to handle. DealHound is the best of both worlds, acting as a companion who finds all the deals and organizes them for you while still giving you control over where and when you shop. Key Features

  1. Smart Shopping Lists: Create shopping lists with automatic deal suggestions to maximize savings.
